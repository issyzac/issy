About
Introducing Awesome iOS UI Kit made with passion for designing and mobile app development. In this UI Kit you will find 120+ High-Quality iOS Templates that will perfectly fit to iPhone Xs Max, Xr, X, Xs and 8. 100% Vector Based, so all screens can be easily resized to fit other iPhone screens. Dimensions 375x812 and 375x667. 

With over 1100+ UI Elements in 10 popular categories you will speed up your design workflow and create awesome trendy design for your apps. Each screen is fully customizable, easy to use and carefully assembled in Sketch, Photoshop, Adobe XD & Figma formats. Popular 8 px Grid. 520+ icons. Free Google fonts Lato & Lora.
